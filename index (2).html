About

"I photograph the moments between moments."

Based in Phoenix, Arizona — and willing to travel — I'm a photographer and videographer drawn to authentic light and real emotion. Whether that's a face caught off-guard, a mountain at dusk, or an athlete mid-flight, I'm there for it.

My work spans portraits, landscape, sports, events, and video. Available for commissions and editorial work.
