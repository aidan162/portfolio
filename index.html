About

Hi, I'm Aidan Arria, a photographer and videographer based in Phoenix, Arizona. Over the past 2.5 years, I've focused on capturing sports, events, and the moments that often go unnoticed in the middle of the action.

My experience includes working with Brophy's The Stampede, covering a wide range of athletic events, as well as creating content for Arcadia Little League and Arcadia High School sports. Whether I'm on the sidelines of a championship game or covering a community event, I aim to tell stories through authentic, well-timed images and video.

Beyond shooting, I enjoy the entire creative process, from planning and filming to editing and delivering a final product that feels polished and purposeful.

I'm always looking for new opportunities and projects. If you're interested in photography, videography, event coverage, sports media, or creative content, I'd love to connect.
